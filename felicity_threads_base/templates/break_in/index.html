Instructions

The official Capture The Flag event for Felicity Threads. Break In is a jeopardy-style CTF event, where you use whatever means at your disposal to win. If you enjoy finding ways in which a system can be broken, this event is for you.
For any query, doubts, suggessions or discrepencies contact via
  • Email: breakin@felicity.iiit.ac.in
Rules  â–¼
  1. Teams :
    • A team, as defined as a collaborative group of individuals, is only allowed to compete under one team name.
    • A team is not allowed to give themselves or another team an unfair advantage.
    • A member of one team is not allowed to share answers with another team. We WILL find out if you do.
    • More than one uniquely named teams working together to secure multiple final round spots will not be tolerated. In this case, both teams will be disqualified.
  2. Competition:
    • In one team, there is a maximum limit of 2 players.
    • CTF will be of 12 hrs. It can be changed if need be.
  3. Infrastructures & Flags:
    • Please do not attack any BreakInCTF infrastructure.
    • We would give extra points to the teams who come forward about the potential mistakes in our Infrastructure.
    • Denial of service attacks will not be tolerated and the team responsible for any kind of DDOS will be terminated immediately.
